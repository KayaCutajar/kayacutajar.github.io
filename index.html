About Me

My name is Kaya Cutajar, a student majoring in Graphic Design, who loves to create art and design. My goal is to be good in my ability and provide good artworks. My passion lies in traditional drawing and creating digital artwork, including posters and sketchbook designs. My goal is to create unique and meaningful illustrations that connect with different audiences and secure a role where I can showcase my skills and abilities.
